Corporate Photography

Refined headshots and brand imagery for founders, creative teams, and companies that want a polished visual identity.

Corporate photography at Aura Photography is designed to make teams look approachable, credible, and visually consistent across websites, press features, proposals, and social channels. We plan around your brand tone so the final set feels cohesive rather than generic.

Sessions can focus on classic executive headshots, environmental portraits, team photography, or a broader collection of brand imagery that includes collaboration scenes, workspace details, and product context. We keep the process efficient so busy teams can move through the session with minimal disruption.

Deliverables you can expect

  • Consistent headshots for leadership and team pages
  • Multiple framing options for web, LinkedIn, and press use
  • Brand-led lighting and background guidance
  • Retouched final selections with fast delivery options
  • Optional team photos and workplace storytelling images

Who this is for

This service works well for startups preparing for launch, firms refreshing team pages, consultants building a premium personal brand, and internal communications teams needing a library of consistent marketing assets.

Session details

  • Format: studio, office, or on-location
  • Best for: teams, founders, speakers, recruiting pages
  • Turnaround: 5 to 10 business days
  • Options: individual, team, or full brand library
