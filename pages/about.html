About Aura Photography

Where artistry meets authenticity

Our Story

Born from a Love of Light

Aura Photography began in 2014 when Elena Vasquez transformed a small Brooklyn loft into a sanctuary for portrait artistry. What started as weekend family sessions grew into a full-service studio trusted by hundreds of clients across New York and beyond.

Our philosophy is simple: photography should feel effortless for you and look extraordinary forever. We combine technical mastery with genuine human connection to create images that become heirlooms.

Lead Photographer

Ethan Carter

Ethan Carter is a professional portrait and wedding photographer with over 12 years of experience capturing life's most meaningful moments. His work is known for its creativity, attention to detail, and natural storytelling, making every photograph timeless and memorable.

Outside of client sessions, Ethan mentors aspiring photographers, explores new photography techniques, and enjoys documenting landscapes and everyday life through his lens.
