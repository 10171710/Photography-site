Wedding Photography

Full-day editorial-style coverage that captures the emotion, detail, and celebration of your wedding day from start to finish.

Our wedding coverage is built around telling the full story of your day, from quiet morning preparations to the last dance. We work discreetly alongside your other vendors, anticipating key moments so nothing important is missed.

Ceremony, portraits, and reception are each treated with a different rhythm: reverent and unobtrusive during the vows, relaxed and collaborative for portraits, and energetic and candid once the celebration begins. The result is a gallery that feels cohesive and true to how the day actually unfolded.

What the session includes

  • Pre-wedding consultation and timeline planning
  • Full-day coverage from preparations to send-off
  • Second photographer available for larger weddings
  • Professionally edited high-resolution gallery
  • Private online gallery for downloads and sharing

Best for

This coverage suits couples who want a complete, unscripted record of their wedding day, from intimate ceremonies to large multi-day celebrations, delivered as a timeless editorial story.

Session details

  • Duration: 8 to 10 hours full-day coverage
  • Ideal locations: ceremony venue, reception hall, outdoor portrait sites
  • Delivery: online gallery within 4 to 6 weeks
  • Add-ons: albums, second shooter, engagement session

Why clients choose it

Couples choose this service for its editorial storytelling style, calm and experienced presence on the day, and a gallery that captures genuine emotion rather than stiff, staged moments.
