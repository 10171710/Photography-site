Meet the Artist

Ethan Carter

With over 12 years of experience behind the camera, Ethan Carter founded Aura Photography with a passion for capturing genuine emotions and unforgettable moments. His creative approach and attention to detail have earned the trust of hundreds of clients for weddings, portraits, and special events.

Based in New York, Ethan specializes in portrait photography, wedding storytelling, and professional event coverage. His goal is to create timeless photographs that preserve life's most meaningful memories.
